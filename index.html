About Us

Professional Civil Engineering & Design-to-Build Firm

Jay Gurudev Enterprises & Construction is a full-service civil engineering and structural development firm headquartered in Bhusawal, Maharashtra. Founded and led by certified Civil Engineers and Professional Interior & Exterior Designers, we bridge the gap between imaginative architectural planning and rock-solid structural execution.

We specialize in end-to-end residential bungalow development, commercial complexes, 2D/3D architectural rendering, JCB earthmoving machinery rentals, and high-grade aggregate material supply. Serving Bhusawal, Jalgaon City, Varangaon, Yawal, Raver, and Jamner, we ensure every project adheres strictly to Indian Standard (IS) structural codes, municipal bylaws, and Vastu principles.
